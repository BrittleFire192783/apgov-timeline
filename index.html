                  What it is

                  A timeline that opens a case card with facts, the constitutional question, the holding, key reasoning, and why it matters for AP Gov.

                  How to study

                  • Pick a year on the timeline.
                  • Read Quick Take and Holding first.
                  • Scan Facts and Reasoning for test-ready language.
                  • Use Connections to link cases by theme.
